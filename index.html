About Us

The Mathematics Department at NUTECH is a leading institution in both pure and applied mathematical research and education. Our department is dedicated to advancing knowledge in core mathematical areas while fostering connections with other disciplines, leading to innovative applications and new insights. Our applied mathematics group focuses on key areas such as data science, artificial intelligence, scientific computing, numerical analysis, and other interdisciplinary fields that merge physical and computational mathematics.

Faculty

Our department boasts a distinguished faculty of approximately 15 members, many of whom have been recognized with prestigious awards and honors. These include national and international accolades such as the Hilbert Medal, National Medals of Science, and various teaching and research prizes. Our faculty members are leaders in their fields and are actively engaged in cutting-edge research that shapes the future of mathematics. They also contribute to national and global scientific communities as members of esteemed organizations.

Post-doctoral Researchers

Our post-doctoral researchers, including NUTECH Fellows, have the opportunity to collaborate with our esteemed faculty and contribute to our vibrant research community. These researchers stay with us for two to three years, with a focus on launching independent research programs while benefiting from a balanced teaching load. We typically host around 30 post-doctoral researchers in our department at any given time.

Students

The Mathematics Department at NUTECH attracts outstanding students from around the globe. Our graduate program is designed to train the next generation of leaders in both academia and industry, with over 100 students currently enrolled. Our undergraduate programs, which include the BS Mathematics and BS Mathematics specialized in Artificial Intelligence, prepare students for top performance in national and international competitions, as well as successful careers in diverse fields. NUTECH students are known for their academic excellence, regularly achieving top positions in national mathematical competitions and contributing to significant research projects.

Environment

Our department thrives in a dynamic academic environment, benefiting from collaborations with other departments and institutions both within and outside of NUTECH. We regularly host seminars, workshops, and colloquia, including the prestigious NUTECH Mathematics Lecture Series, which features distinguished speakers on the latest developments in mathematical research. Our strong ties with industry and research institutes also provide our students and faculty with unique opportunities for collaboration and innovation.

Staff

Our dedicated staff plays a crucial role in the success of the Mathematics Department. They manage the day-to-day operations of the department, support academic services, and ensure that our students and faculty have the resources they need to excel. Whether assisting with administrative tasks or supporting research initiatives, our staff members are an integral part of our community.
