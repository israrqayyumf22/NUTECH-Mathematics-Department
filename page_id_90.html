Important Dates for the Upcoming Academic Year

As we approach the new academic year, here are some key dates to remember:

  • Fall Semester Start: September 10, 2024
  • Mid-Semester Break: November 5-9, 2024
  • Final Exams Begin: December 15, 2024
  • Winter Break: December 25, 2024 - January 10, 2025

Make sure to mark these dates on your calendar and plan accordingly. For more details, please contact the Academic Office.

University Health and Safety Guidelines

In light of recent health advisories, NUTECH has updated its health and safety guidelines to ensure the well-being of all students, faculty, and staff. Key measures include:

  • Mandatory wearing of masks in all indoor spaces.
  • Regular sanitation of campus facilities.
  • Daily health screenings at campus entrances.
  • Social distancing protocols in classrooms and common areas.

Please familiarize yourself with the full guidelines on the University Health and Safety page.

New Research Opportunities in AI and Data Science

We are excited to announce new research opportunities in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. Students and faculty are encouraged to participate in the following programs:

  • AI Research Grant Program: Apply for research grants focused on innovative AI projects. Submission deadline: October 1, 2024.
  • Data Science Internship Program: Gain hands-on experience with leading tech companies through our new internship partnerships. Application period: September 15 - October 15, 2024.
  • Research Symposium: Present your research findings and network with experts at our annual symposium. Date: November 20, 2024.

For more details, visit the Research Opportunities page.
