Research Labs

Foster Innovation – Advance Knowledge – Drive Progress!

NUTECH is committed to providing state-of-the-art facilities and resources to support cutting-edge research and academic excellence. Our research labs are designed to offer a conducive environment for exploration, experimentation, and discovery across various disciplines. Below is an overview of the Computer Labs available at NUTECH.

Computer Labs at NUTECH

Our computer labs are equipped with the latest technology and software to support both academic and research activities. They are designed to facilitate a range of computing needs, from basic programming to advanced data analysis and simulation. Here’s a detailed overview:

1. General Computing Lab

The General Computing Lab is equipped with modern PCs that provide students with access to standard software and development tools. This lab supports a variety of courses and activities, including programming, web development, and general computing tasks.

  • Computers: 30 high-performance PCs
  • Software: Python, Java, C++, Visual Studio, MS Office, etc.
  • Facilities: High-speed internet, projectors, and printing services

2. Advanced Research Lab

This lab is dedicated to advanced research activities and is equipped with high-end workstations capable of handling complex computations and simulations. It supports research in areas such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science.

  • Computers: 15 workstations with high-end GPUs
  • Software: TensorFlow, PyTorch, MATLAB, R, SPSS, etc.
  • Facilities: High-speed networking, data storage solutions, and visualization tools

3. Networking and Security Lab

The Networking and Security Lab provides resources for studying and testing network configurations, cybersecurity protocols, and IT infrastructure. It is equipped with specialized hardware and software to simulate real-world networking environments.

  • Computers: 20 PCs with network simulation tools
  • Software: Cisco Packet Tracer, Wireshark, Kali Linux, etc.
  • Facilities: Network simulation equipment, security testing tools, and dedicated servers

4. Multimedia and Graphics Lab

This lab focuses on multimedia applications, including graphics design, animation, and video editing. It provides students and researchers with the tools needed to create high-quality multimedia content.

  • Computers: 25 PCs with advanced graphics capabilities
  • Software: Adobe Creative Suite, Blender, AutoCAD, etc.
  • Facilities: High-resolution monitors, graphic tablets, and audio-visual equipment

5. Virtual Reality (VR) Lab

The VR Lab is dedicated to research and development in virtual reality technologies. It offers immersive experiences and development environments for VR applications, simulations, and interactive content.

  • Computers: 10 PCs with VR-capable hardware
  • Software: Unity, Unreal Engine, Oculus SDK, etc.
  • Facilities: VR headsets, motion sensors, and interactive controllers

6. Data Science and Analytics Lab

This lab is tailored for data science research and analytics. It provides tools and resources for handling large datasets, performing statistical analysis, and developing data-driven models.

  • Computers: 15 PCs with high data processing capabilities
  • Software: Hadoop, Spark, Tableau, Excel, etc.
  • Facilities: Data visualization tools, large storage capacity, and high-speed data access

For more information about our research labs, or to book a lab for your research or academic work, please contact the Research Office or visit our Research Labs Booking Portal.
