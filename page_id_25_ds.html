BS Mathematics with Specialization in Data Science Curriculum

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

The graduate of BS Mathematics with a specialization in Data Science is expected to accomplish the following PEOs:

  • PEO - 1: Graduates will apply their mathematical and statistical knowledge to analyze complex data and provide actionable insights.
  • PEO - 2: Graduates will demonstrate proficiency in using advanced data science tools and techniques to solve real-world problems.
  • PEO - 3: Graduates will contribute to interdisciplinary teams and effectively communicate data-driven solutions in various professional settings.
  • PEO - 4: Graduates will engage in lifelong learning to keep pace with emerging trends and technologies in data science.

Program Learning Objectives (PLOs)

The PLOs are specific goals outlining what students are expected to learn and achieve by graduation:

  • PLO - 1: Understand and apply fundamental concepts of mathematics, statistics, and computer science to solve data-related problems.
  • PLO - 2: Develop skills in data collection, cleaning, and visualization to support data-driven decision-making.
  • PLO - 3: Utilize machine learning algorithms and statistical models to analyze large datasets and make predictions.
  • PLO - 4: Implement data science methodologies and tools, including programming languages such as Python and R, for data analysis and modeling.
  • PLO - 5: Communicate complex data insights and analytical results effectively to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
  • PLO - 6: Apply ethical considerations and best practices in data handling and analysis to ensure data privacy and integrity.
  • PLO - 7: Engage in continuous professional development to adapt to advancements in data science and related technologies.
